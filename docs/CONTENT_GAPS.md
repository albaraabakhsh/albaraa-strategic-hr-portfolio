# Content gaps

The following information is intentionally omitted until independently verified:

- Current and previous employment dates and earlier employer names.
- University name and graduation year.
- KPI Professional issuer, completion date, and credential number.
- LinkedIn, GitHub, X, WhatsApp, and meeting links.
- Named recommendations and quotations.
- Quantified outcomes such as employee counts, processing volumes, time savings, or percentages.
- A public résumé PDF containing verified dates and institutions.

These gaps do not block publication. They should be added only with primary evidence and matching Arabic and English content.
